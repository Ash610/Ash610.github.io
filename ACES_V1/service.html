Preventative Maintenance

You'll need some help with staying ahead of equipment breaking down. The only way to stay ahead of this issue is by proactively checking your assets. Preventative Maintenance programs(PM's) are a cost effective strategy for providing maintence management control in your facility. Compactors and balers aging presuure settings and hydraulic fluit levels should be cheked regulary and if necessary changed. A yearly inspection of the eintre machine can save a considerable amount of money in the long run. Metal machines with moving parts hould be checked for metal fatigue. Proper maintenance will help extend the life of your baler and compactor.

Professional cleaning is another service offered by ACES Preventative Maintenance. Once a year, our technician will internally clean, deodorize and disinfect the compactor and chute. Included in the process is cleaning of the hopper door and adjusting the alignment and tension if needed.

The Benefits of Preventative Maintenance

  • Proactively manage your assets reducing downtime
  • Extend the life of your equipment and know its function at its best.
  • Reduce hauling costs with proper pressure settings
